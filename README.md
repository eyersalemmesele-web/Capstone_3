# Capstone_3: EmporiUm Sales Analysis with Power BI

## Project Overview
This Power BI report analyzes sales performance for EmporiUm, a virtual student bookstore, 
covering in-store and online sales from 2022 through 2025.

## Data Sources
- Store Sales: 335,129 in-store transactions across 111 locations in 10+ states
- Online Sales: 127,965 online transactions shipped to 29 states
- Products: 669 products across 6 categories
- Store Locations: 111 store locations

## Key Findings
- Maryland, Colorado, and Massachusetts are the top-performing states for in-store sales
- Technology & Accessories and Stationery are the highest-revenue categories
- Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone is the top-selling general audience book
- Sales show clear seasonal patterns tied to the academic calendar

## Report Pages
1. **Sales Overview** — Sales trend, category performance, and state breakdown
2. **Books & Top Performers** — Top-selling books with author names
